Questions

Common questions families may have

What does a doula do?

A doula provides emotional, physical, and informational support during pregnancy, labor, birth, and postpartum recovery.

Do you replace a doctor or midwife?

No. A doula complements your medical team by focusing on comfort, education, practical support, and care centered on your preferences.
